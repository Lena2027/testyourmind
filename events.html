The Big Three (Must-Visit)

SEPT - OCT 2026

Oktoberfest Munich

The world's largest Volksfest. Pro tip: Book your beer tent seats at least 6 months in advance. Experience the ultimate Bavarian culture with traditional Lederhosen and Dirndl.

FEB 2026 (Peak)

Cologne Carnival

Known as the "Fifth Season". The celebration starts on Nov 11th but peaks in February with Rose Monday parades. Don't forget your costume and the cry of "Kölle Alaaf!"

FEB 2026

Berlinale Film Festival

A paradise for art and culture lovers. One of the most prestigious film festivals in the world, bringing global stars and groundbreaking cinema to the heart of Berlin.

Seasonal Highlights

Christmas Markets (Weihnachtsmärkte)

From late November to Dec 24th. Compare the historic Nuremberg Christkindlesmarkt, Dresden's Striezelmarkt, and the medieval Rothenburg ob der Tauber markets.

Wine Festivals (Weinfeste)

August to September. Explore the scenic Rhine and Mosel regions (Hessen, Rhineland-Pfalz) during their famous wine harvest festivals.

Traditional Holiday Events

Easter Fires (Osterfeuer): Look for traditional bonfires in northern Germany (Lower Saxony, Schleswig-Holstein) on Easter Saturday.

Vatertag (Father's Day): Watch for "Bollerwagen" processions across Berlin and rural Brandenburg on Ascension Day.

German Unity Day 2026: Stay tuned for the host city announcement for the 2026 central celebrations (Bürgerfest).
