Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Test Your Mind ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website at testyourmind.io (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our contact form, you may voluntarily provide your name and email address. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not store test answers or individual test results on our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, including:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate our site effectively. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies: Used via Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site and improve our content. Data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Advertising cookies: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

2. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads on our site. These cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Site and its content
  • Respond to your contact form inquiries
  • Analyze site usage and traffic patterns through Google Analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for any purpose other than those described above.

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' standard retention settings. We do not retain individual test responses or results.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@testyourmind.io.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@testyourmind.io.
