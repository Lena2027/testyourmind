2027 German Holiday Preview

Smart planners look ahead. Here is your early guide to the most important public holidays in Germany for 2027.

Why Plan for 2027 Now?

In Germany, vacation days are highly prized, and securing the best "Brückentage" (bridge days) requires booking months in advance. By looking at 2027 now, you can submit your annual leave requests before your colleagues.

Key Dates to Watch in 2027 (National)

  • New Year's Day: Friday, Jan 1, 2027 (Long weekend!)
  • Good Friday: March 26, 2027
  • Easter Monday: March 29, 2027
  • Labor Day: Saturday, May 1, 2027 (Falls on a weekend)
  • Ascension Day: Thursday, May 6, 2027 (Take Friday off!)
  • Whit Monday: May 17, 2027
  • German Unity Day: Sunday, Oct 3, 2027
  • Christmas: Sat Dec 25 & Sun Dec 26, 2027 (Bad year for Christmas holidays)

Analysis: 2027 vs 2026

2027 is unfortunately not the best year for employees, as several fixed-date holidays (like May 1st, Oct 3rd, and Christmas) fall on weekends. This means you will need to rely more heavily on the movable Easter-based holidays to maximize your rest.
