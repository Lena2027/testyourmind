About Our Project

Dedicated to providing accurate, accessible, and comprehensive holiday data for residents and travelers in Germany.

Our Mission

Navigating the complex system of German public holidays—with its national rules and state-by-state variations—can be frustrating. Our mission is to simplify this process. Whether you are planning a vacation, organizing business meetings, or just trying to figure out if the supermarket is open today, we provide the reliable data you need.

Data Sources & Accuracy

Last Updated: March 2026

Trust is our top priority. The dates and regulations presented on this website are meticulously cross-referenced with official publications from the Federal Statistical Office of Germany (Destatis) and individual state legislation. We ensure that our interactive calendar reflects the most current legal holiday status across all 16 Bundesländer.

Why We Created This Tool

As expats who once struggled with unexpectedly closed grocery stores on 'Fronleichnam', we realized the need for a user-friendly, English-language tool that not only lists dates but explains the why and where behind each German holiday. This project is built by locals and expats, for everyone.
